Refund Policy

Easy returns and hassle-free refunds for your peace of mind

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Return Window

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery for most products. The return window starts from the date you receive your order, not from the date of purchase.

2. Eligible Items for Return

The following items are eligible for return:

  • Products in original, unused condition
  • Items with original packaging and tags attached
  • Products with all accessories and manuals included
  • Items that are not damaged due to misuse

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Perishable goods
  • Items damaged due to misuse or normal wear and tear
  • Products without original packaging or tags
  • Items purchased on clearance or final sale
  • Gift cards and digital products

4. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, follow these simple steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email us at info@shopease.com or call +91 98765 43210
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, product details, and reason for return
  3. Get Approval: We'll review your request and provide a return authorization
  4. Pack the Item: Securely pack the item in its original packaging
  5. Schedule Pickup: We'll arrange free pickup from your address

5. Return Shipping

We provide FREE return pickup for all eligible returns within India. Our logistics partner will collect the item from your doorstep at no additional cost to you.

6. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Inspection: We'll inspect the item within 2-3 business days
  • Approval: If approved, we'll process your refund
  • Refund Timeline: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Payment Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method

7. Refund Timeline by Payment Method

Payment Method Refund Timeline
Credit/Debit Card 5-7 business days
Net Banking 5-7 business days
UPI 1-3 business days
Digital Wallets 1-3 business days
Cash on Delivery 7-10 business days (Bank Transfer)

8. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, color, or model:

  • Return the original item following our return process
  • Place a new order for the desired item
  • Once your return is processed, you'll receive a full refund

9. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damage
  • We'll expedite the return process
  • You may be eligible for a replacement or full refund
  • No questions asked for manufacturing defects

10. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following cases:

  • Items returned without original packaging
  • Products showing signs of use beyond normal inspection
  • Items missing accessories or manuals
  • Returned items damaged during return shipping (not due to our packaging)

11. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order before it ships:

  • Before Processing: Full refund within 24 hours
  • After Processing: Order cannot be cancelled, but you can return after delivery
  • Prepaid Orders: Refund processed within 5-7 business days
  • COD Orders: No charges if cancelled before shipping

12. Quality Assurance

All returned items undergo quality inspection to ensure they meet our standards for resale. Items that don't pass inspection may receive partial refunds or may not be eligible for refund.

14. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our resolution of your return or refund request, you may:

  • Escalate the issue to our customer service manager
  • File a complaint with consumer protection authorities
  • Seek resolution through appropriate legal channels
